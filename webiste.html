About us Description

We're your one-stop-shop for playing tennis in Newbury and Thatcham! We offer pay & play and affordable household memberships that make it super easy to pick up a racket. We aim to make tennis easily accessible with low charges, professional organisation and engaging activities for the local community. Our focus is to promote tennis throughout the year for all age groups, both adults and children, through social play, internal competition, matches, and professional coaching to any player who wants to improve their game.
